Slumper

Sleep isn’t just important—it’s your body’s free trial of death, but with breakfast afterward. Skip it too often, and suddenly you’re paying interest in yawns, brain fog, and questionable life choices. So go to bed—your future self will thank you… after the nap.

Pro's

  • Free escape room for 8 hours.
  • Brain does nightly software updates.
  • Dreams = free Netflix, but with worse plotlines.
  • Zero chance of saying something embarrassing (unless you sleep talk).
  • Body fixes itself like it’s on auto-repair mode.

Con's

  • Takes up valuable scrolling time.
  • Alarm clocks exist.
  • Random dreams where you’re late to an exam you finished 5 years ago.
  • Can’t eat snacks while unconscious.
  • Waking up and realizing it’s Monday again.
